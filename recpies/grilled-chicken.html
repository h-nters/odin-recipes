Grilled Chicken w/Mayo Marinade

Description

This marinade is based off of Ethan Cheblowski's chicken.

Ingredients:

  • Mayo
  • Black Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Garlic Salt
  • Red Pepper Chili Flake

Steps:

  1. Get 4 heaping tablespoons of mayo, add to a medium sized bowl.
  2. Add black pepper to taste.
  3. Add a tablespoon of oreagno.
  4. Add a teaspoon of garlic salt.
  5. Add your preffered amount of chili flakes.
  6. Mix in a bowl, apply to chicken breasts (2-4).
  7. Throw on top of grill, cook for 6-9 minutes on each side depending on size.
  8. Serve with your choice of vegetables.
